Garlic Naan

from Satyam's myCookbook

8 min

Flavor packed slow cooker cheesy beef nacho soup! Enjoy the taste of nachos in a comforting soup, perfect for any night of the week!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground beef​
  • 15 oz Black beans
  • 15 oz Corn
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 cup Green bell pepper
  • 1 Green onions
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 cup Yellow onion​
  • 3 1/2 cup Chicken or beef broth
  • 3 tbsp All purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Black pepper, ground
  • 1 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt​
  • 8 oz Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup Heavy cream​
  • 15oz petite diced tomatotes, drained
